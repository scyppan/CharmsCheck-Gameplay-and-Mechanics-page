Personality Traits

Each character gets between zero and four Personality Traits (shortened to traits) when they are born. These cannot change and you will never receive more. They are fairly simple and straightforward bonuses, typically, to non-casting skills or to casting subtypes.

  • Star gazer: +3 to Astronomy
  • Bookworm: +3 to History
  • Animal lover: +1 to Creatures
  • People person: +1 to Social Skills
  • Clairvoyant: +3 to Divination
  • Navigator: +2 to Flying
  • Observant: +1 to Perception
  • Green thumb: +3 to Herbology
  • Frugal: +9 sickles per month to your allowance (under 17); +2 Galleons per month to your salary (when employed and 17 or older)
  • Curious: +1 to Arithmancy
  • Inventive: +1 to Artificing
  • Runologist: +2 to Runes
  • Protective: +3 to shielding casts
  • Caring: +2 to any healing casts or proficiency attempts
  • Environmentalist: +3 to environmental casts
  • Needler: +3 to mental casts
  • Contrarian: +2 to counterspell casts
  • Resistant: +3 to repelling casts
  • Controlling: +2 to controlling casts
  • Supportive: +3 to enhancing casts
  • Ouster: +3 to banishing casts
  • Secretive: +3 to concealing casts
  • Crafty: +3 to enchanting casts
