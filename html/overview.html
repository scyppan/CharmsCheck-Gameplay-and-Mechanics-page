Overview

Charms Check is a role-playing game set in the Wizarding World, designed for semi-virtual sessions that rely on bespoke digital tools for smooth play. A d10 engine and action-based time mechanics break adventures into scene-like beats that highlight story, relationships, and exploration while day-to-day school life unfolds quietly in the background.
