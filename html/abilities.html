Abilities

Abilities represent a character’s broad magical interests or persuasions. Each ability governs a fixed set of skills.

  • Power: Raw magical force. A character with high Power can channel great energy into spells, overwhelming resistances and extending effects. Skills: Charms, Transfiguration, Defense, Dark Arts. Note: All spell-casting is performed through these Power-linked skills—if you want to sling spells, invest here.
  • Erudition: Scholarly depth and theoretical insight. High Erudition reflects fluency in runes, numerics, history, and cross-cultural lore. Skills: Arithmancy, Ancient Runes, History of Magic, Muggle Studies.
  • Panache: Creative flair and technical daring. Characters strong in Panache thrive in disciplines that reward experimentation, precision, and bold maneuvering. Skills: Potions, Alchemy, Artificing, Flying, Herbology.
  • Naturalism: Attunement to living beings and cosmic rhythms. High Naturalism brings keen awareness of creatures, celestial patterns, future portents, and social currents. Skills: Care of Magical Creatures, Astronomy, Divination, Perception, Social skills.
